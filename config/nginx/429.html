429

Too Many Requests

You have exceeded the rate limit for this service. Please slow down your requests.

Rate Limits

  • API requests: 10 per second
  • Query requests: 20 per second
  • Authentication: 5 per minute
  • Concurrent connections: 10 per IP
⏱️ Please wait a moment before retrying your request.

What can you do?

  • Wait a few seconds and try again
  • Reduce the frequency of your requests
  • Implement exponential backoff in your client
  • Contact support if you need higher limits

If you believe this is an error, please contact your system administrator.
